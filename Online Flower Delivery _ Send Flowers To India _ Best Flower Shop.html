Types of Flower Available For Every Special Occasion

Looking for the perfect blooms for every special occasion? Look no further than FlowerAura! Our online flower delivery service makes it easy to send fresh, vibrant flowers to your loved ones in India. We have a blissful range of fresh flowers to pick from.

Roses: Roses are the most prevalent flowers for gifting on special occasions. Red roses tend to indicate love and romance, while fresh pink roses signify affection. Yellow roses represent lively friendship, and white roses are associated with purity and innocence.

Carnations: Carnations are another popular flower option. They come in a variety of colours, and each colour has a different meaning. Red carnations represent love, while white carnations signify purity and luck.

Lilies: Lilies are elegant flowers that come in different shades of white, pink, and yellow. They symbolise purity, innocence, and beauty.

Orchids: Orchids are exotic flowers that come in various colours and shapes. They are associated with luxury, beauty, and strength.

Mixed Flowers: Mixed flowers are a great way to add a pop of colour and variety to any bouquet or arrangement. With their different sizes, shapes, and colours, mixed flowers create a stunning and unique display that will delight anyone who receives them.

Gerberas: Gerberas are colourful flowers that come in different hues of pink, orange, yellow, and red. They represent happiness, joy, and cheerfulness.

No matter what the occasion, FlowerAura has the perfect blooms to express your love, gratitude, and appreciation.
